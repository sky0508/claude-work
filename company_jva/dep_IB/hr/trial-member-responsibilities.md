# IB Trial Member — Responsibilities

> For: Trial member joining March 2026
> Effective: From start of trial period
> Reviewed by: IB team (Sora + Nahi)

---

## Overview

As a trial member of JVA Internship Board (IB), you will be responsible for the day-to-day operations that connect students with companies. Start with #1 and #2, then expand into #3 and #4 as you get comfortable.

---

## Responsibilities (in priority order)

### 1. Student Community Management

Be the main point of contact for students in the IB LINE community.

- Monitor the LINE group daily and respond to student questions and messages
- Send weekly LINE broadcasts featuring active job postings
- Maintain a welcoming and professional tone in all communications
- Flag any issues or unusual requests to the IB team lead

> **Goal**: Students feel heard, informed, and motivated to apply.

---

### 2. Application Management

Handle the full flow from the moment a student applies to the point a company receives their CV.

**Flow:**
```
Student submits application (Google Form)
        ↓
Check basic eligibility
  · Is the student based in Tokyo or able to commute?
  · Do they have valid work authorization in Japan?
        ↓
If eligible → record in tracking spreadsheet
        ↓
Forward CV to company contact (via automated email or manually)
        ↓
Monitor company response
  · No reply after 4 business days → alert the team
        ↓
Update application status: Sent → Under Review → Interview → Match / Rejected
```

> **Goal**: Every application is processed within 48 hours. No CV falls through the cracks. Companies receive clean, relevant applications.

---

### 3. Company Research & Outreach Support

Help grow the company side of IB by finding and approaching new startups.

- Research potential companies that match our student profile (English-friendly, flexible hours, entry-level roles)
- Build a prospect list with company name, contact person, and email
- Draft outreach messages for team review before sending
- Log all outreach activity and responses in the company pipeline tracker

> **Goal**: Keep the company pipeline growing with quality listings.

---

### 4. Student Acquisition Strategy

Think proactively about how to grow the student community.

- Identify channels and tactics to attract more students to IB (LinkedIn, university communities, events, etc.)
- Propose content ideas or campaigns that could drive LINE registrations
- Collaborate with the team to test and iterate on what works

> **Goal**: Contribute ideas and execution to grow our student base beyond the current LinkedIn-driven approach.

---

## What Success Looks Like (Trial Period)

| Responsibility | Success Metric |
|----------------|---------------|
| #1 Student Community | Response time < 24h, weekly post goes out on schedule |
| #2 Application Management | All applications processed within 48h, zero missed CVs |
| #3 Company Outreach | 5+ companies researched and added to prospect list |
| #4 Student Acquisition | At least 1 new channel or tactic proposed and tested |
