### [Section 2] What is IB? — 5 min

**Slide: What is Internship Board?**

A platform that connects international students in Japan with global startups — through a simple, accessible, and English-friendly experience.

**Slide: Our Mission**

> *"Finding an internship in Japan shouldn't be this hard. We're changing that."*

**Slide: Who We Serve**

| | Students | Companies |
|--|----------|-----------|
| **Primary** | International students enrolled at Japanese universities | Global startups in Japan |
| **Secondary** | Exchange students in Japan | English-friendly startups looking to expand globally |
| **Also** | Japanese students who want to work at global startups | |

**Slide: The Win-Win**

### [Section 6] How We Work — 8 min

**Slide: The Operation Flow**

```
Student joins LINE
        ↓
Browses listings on Notion
        ↓
Applies via Google Form (with CV)
        ↓
IB team forwards CV to company
        ↓
Company reviews & schedules interview
        ↓
Interview takes place
        ↓
Match confirmed → Internship starts ✓
```

**Slide: Tools We Use**

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| LINE Official Account | Student community & weekly broadcasts |
| Notion | Job listings, application tracking, workspace |
| Google Sheets | Student DB, company DB, application tracker |
| Gmail | Company communication |
