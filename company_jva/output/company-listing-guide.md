# JVA Internship Board — Company Listing Guide

> Version: 1.0 | Created: 2026-03-16
> 対象: 掲載企業向け（外部配布用）

---

## What is JVA IB?

JVA Internship Board (IB) is an internship matching platform connecting startups with global university students based in Japan. We distribute your internship listing to our LINE community and manage the application flow end-to-end.

**Our student pool at a glance:**

| | |
|---|---|
| English level | 80%+ business to native |
| Japanese level | Mostly N3–N5 (beginner to intermediate) |
| Background | 70% business / economics / liberal arts |
| Universities | UTokyo, Keio, Waseda, Tokyo Tech, and more |

---

## How to List a Position

```
Meeting with IB team
        ↓
Submit Job Card form
        ↓
IB creates & reviews your listing
        ↓
Distributed to LINE community
        ↓
Applications arrive via email
```

---

## What to Include in Your Job Card

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| Position title | Clear and specific |
| Key responsibilities | 3–5 bullet points |
| English requirement | Business / Native / Not required |
| Japanese requirement | Not required / N3+ / Business |
| Weekly hours | **15–25h recommended** |
| Compensation | ¥/h or monthly |
| Location | In-person (Tokyo) / Remote / Hybrid |
| Work authorization required | Yes / No |
| Ideal candidate | 2–3 sentences |

> **Tips for a higher match rate:**
> - Keep weekly hours at 15–25h (35–40h significantly reduces the applicant pool)
> - If Japanese is not essential, set it as "Not required" — this opens up 80% of our students
> - Remote-friendly roles tend to receive more applications

---

## Platform Rules

### 1. Respond to applicants within 4 business days

When you receive a CV, please reply within **4 business days** — even if it's just to confirm receipt or decline.

| Situation | What happens |
|---|---|
| No response after 4 business days | IB sends a reminder |
| No response after a second reminder | Listing temporarily suspended |
| Repeated non-response | Listing removed until resolved |

### 2. Keep us updated at every stage

Please update IB within **4 business days** at each stage of the selection process.

> CV reviewed → Interview scheduled → Final decision
> Just a one-line message is enough — we'll take it from there.

---

## What Happens After You List

1. Applications arrive in your inbox automatically
2. Review the CV and reply to the applicant directly
3. Let IB know the outcome at each stage
4. IB follows up monthly to check if your listing is still active

We're here to make the process as smooth as possible — reach out anytime if you have questions.

# 【日本語版】JVA IB — 企業掲載ガイド

## JVA IBとは

JVA Internship Board（IB）は、スタートアップとグローバル学生をつなぐインターンシップマッチングプラットフォームです。掲載から応募受付まで、IBがサポートします。

**学生プロフィール**

| | |
|---|---|
| 英語力 | 80%以上がビジネス〜ネイティブレベル |
| 日本語力 | 約80%がN3〜N5（初級〜中級） |
| 専攻 | 約70%がビジネス・経済・リベラルアーツ系 |
| 在籍大学 | 東京大学・慶應・早稲田・東京工業大学など |

---

## 掲載の流れ

ミーティング → Job Cardフォーム提出 → IB審査・掲載 → LINE配信 → 応募受付

---

## Job Cardに書いてほしいこと

職種・業務内容（3〜5点）・英語/日本語レベル・週稼働時間（**15〜25h推奨**）・報酬・勤務形態・就労資格の要否・求める人物像

> **マッチ率を上げるコツ:** 週35h超・日本語必須・東京出社のみは応募者が大幅に減ります。

---

## プラットフォームルール

### 1. CV受領後4営業日以内に返信

合否・保留いずれでも構いません。一言でOKです。

| 状況 | 対応 |
|---|---|
| 4営業日経過・返信なし | IBよりリマインド |
| 2回リマインド後も返信なし | 掲載一時停止 |
| 繰り返し無返信 | 掲載削除 |

### 2. 選考の進捗を都度4営業日以内に連絡

書類通過・面接設定・最終結果など、**各ステージで4営業日以内**にIBへご連絡ください。

> 一言メッセージで構いません。IBが記録・管理します。

---

## 掲載後の流れ

1. 応募がメールで届く
2. 学生に直接返信
3. 各ステージの結果をIBに連絡
4. IBが月次で掲載状況を確認

ご不明な点はいつでもご連絡ください！
