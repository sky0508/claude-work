# Manus Prompt: Company Listing Guide
> Created: 2026-03-16

---

```
Create a 7-slide company listing guide presentation in English.

Purpose: A guide for startups on how to list an internship position on JVA IB. Handed to companies after an initial meeting. Tone: welcoming and professional.

Slide structure:
1. Cover
   - Title: "JVA Internship Board — Company Listing Guide"
   - Subtitle: "Everything you need to know to get started"

2. What is JVA IB?
   - Three columns:
     - Col 1 "What We Do": Free internship matching platform connecting startups with global university students in Japan. We handle distribution and application management end-to-end.
     - Col 2 "Our Students": English 80%+ business/native · Japanese mostly N3–N5 · 70% business/econ/liberal arts · UTokyo, Keio, Waseda, Tokyo Tech and more
     - Col 3 "Why It Works": Curated global talent pool · No recruitment cost · Managed application process · Active community of 59+ students

3. How It Works
   - Horizontal step flow (5 steps):
     1. Meeting with IB team
     2. Submit Job Card form
     3. IB creates & reviews your listing
     4. Distributed to LINE community
     5. Applications arrive in your inbox

4. What to Include in Your Job Card
   - List of fields with notes:
     - Position title — Clear and specific
     - Key responsibilities — 3–5 bullet points
     - English requirement — Business / Native / Not required
     - Japanese requirement — Not required / N3+ / Business
     - Weekly hours — 15–25h recommended
     - Compensation — ¥/h or monthly
     - Location — In-person (Tokyo) / Remote / Hybrid
     - Work authorization required — Yes / No
     - Ideal candidate — 2–3 sentences
   - Tip: "15–25h/week and remote-friendly = significantly more applicants"

5. Platform Rules
   - Rule 1 "Respond within 4 business days":
     When you receive a CV, reply within 4 business days — even just to confirm or decline.
     - No response after 4 business days → IB sends a reminder
     - No response after 2nd reminder → Listing temporarily suspended
     - Repeated non-response → Listing removed
   - Rule 2 "Report selection outcomes":
     Keep us updated within 4 business days at every stage.

6. What Happens After You List
   - Timeline (4 steps):
     1. Applications arrive automatically in your inbox
     2. Review CV and reply to the applicant directly
     3. Report outcome to IB (Pass/Reject → Offer/No offer)
     4. IB follows up monthly to check if your listing is still active

7. Ending
   - Message: "Ready to find your next intern?"
   - Contact: [IB contact info]

Include speaker notes on every slide.
Export as .pptx
```
